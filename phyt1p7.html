1.5 Moments

As discussed earlier, a force can change the state of the object. So, if the object for example is moving, the force could change its direction or even turn it. The turning effect caused by force is called moment. Moments can be seen in everyday life examples such as the seesaw, scissors and wheelbarrow etc.
